ABOUT

I recently graduated from UC Berkeley with a degree in Computer Science and enjoy full-stack/back-end web development, educational policy, and using technology for social good. You'll always catch me listening to Jhene Aiko, Oh Wonder, or The Internet while sipping coffee from my french press.
